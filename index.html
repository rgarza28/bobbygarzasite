About Me


My family and friends call me Bobby. I currently live in Grapevine Texas and have been in the DFW area my entire life. I went to Lewisville High School and spent some time studying Civil Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington. I have always been interested in technology wherther it has to do with the hardware or software aspect of it. Solving problems has been the main reasoning for sticking to a technology based career. Technology not only solves problems but it also optimizes how quickly probelems can be solved.

I have worked in the computer and mobile repair industry for 10 years now. I started as a DIY'er and eventually progressed into a full fledged technician working in retail. I quickly rose into the support role of Master Technicina supporting other stores and techs when they encountered issues. I then progressed to the role of trainer teaching new technicians how to repair devices and prevent common mistakes.


I look towards the future with a bigger focus on software based technology versus hardware. In this digital age software that becomes obsolete can be updated with the push of a button and with ease.
